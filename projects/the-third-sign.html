Project 04 / Concept collection

The Third Sign

A Gemini-inspired collection exploring dual nature, communication, mystique, and versatility through a structured menswear vocabulary and a blue-driven tonal world.

Theme

The project draws from celestial atmosphere, the twin symbolism of Castor and Pollux, and the restless adaptability often associated with Gemini. The result is poised between calm and motion.

Tailoring

Duality becomes visible through layered structure and tonal contrast.

Deep blues, wrap closures, asymmetry, and varied proportion allow the collection to feel polished without becoming rigid. It reads as business-casual, but with a more atmospheric personality.

Visual character

The palette stays anchored in cool neutrals and blue tonalities, creating a mood of intelligence, night, restraint, and contemporary elegance.

Identity

This project treats clothing as an expression of layered personality. Each look suggests movement between sides of self rather than a single fixed image.
