Selected work

Case studies on the page

Project 01

Wrapora

A recovery-wear collection shaped by cocoon and wrap silhouettes. The project focuses on softness, breathable stretch, gentle compression, warmth, and a calm transition from workout to rest.

Recovery wear Cocoon silhouettes Polyester spandex knits

Design direction

Oversized collars, thumb-hole cuffs, shawl wraps, high-low hems, wide sleeves, and fluid joggers create a protective after-training layer.

Material story

Soft-touch jersey and polyester spandex knit support breathability, stretch, easy care, and a comfortable medium-weight drape.

Project 02

Ver Forme

A sustainable semi-formal collection for Gen Z, blending export-house precision with eco-conscious material choices, modular design, clean lines, and digital-first shopping behavior.

Sustainable semi-formal Gen Z consumer Suiting materials

Consumer lens

Built for globally connected students and early-career professionals who value versatility, transparency, ethical practice, and adaptable wardrobes.

Collection logic

The work pairs clean silhouettes with eco-friendly materials including organic cotton, recycled polyester, upcycled fabrics, and zero-waste thinking.

Project 03

Oceanic Capsule

A gender-neutral kidswear capsule, leaning toward boys' styles, inspired by ocean calm, marine life, nautical references, and the comfort needs of active children aged 4-8.

Kidswear Spring/Summer Washable knits

Play and function

Comfortable hoods, raglan sleeves, flexible cuffs, and unrestricted movement anchor the garments for active daily use.

Fabric palette

Melange jersey, single jersey, rib knit, terry knit, and full-needle rib bring softness, breathability, stretch, and cozy structure.

Project 04

The Third Sign

A Gemini-inspired collection based on dual nature, versatility, celestial atmosphere, ancient Greek and Babylonian references, and the bond of Castor and Pollux.

Gemini concept Celestial palette Business casual

Concept note

Deep blue tonalities, integrated layering, raw yarn textures, and structured tailoring create a Gen Z professional wardrobe with quiet confidence.

Mood

The collection sits between mystique and utility: starry, adaptive, communicative, and refined.

Project 05

NU KAWA

A luxury leather accessories brand shaped around natural leather, handcrafted precision, timeless elegance, ethical sourcing, and a confident global customer.

Luxury leather Brand identity Market gap analysis

Brand stance

NU KAWA positions natural leather as durable, biodegradable, expressive, and crafted for meaningful luxury rather than fast trend cycles.

Audience

Designed for urban, fashion-conscious individuals who prefer quality over trends and connect with sustainability, individuality, and exclusivity.

Project 06

Alder & Ash

A premium menswear concept for conscious Gen Z professionals, built on quiet luxury, natural fibers, ethical sourcing, digital-first marketing, and investment-quality wardrobe pieces.

Menswear strategy Quiet luxury Financial planning

Technical development
