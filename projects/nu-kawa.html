Project 05 / Accessories brand

NU KAWA

A luxury leather accessories brand built around natural material, personal confidence, handcrafted precision, and a quieter, more rooted version of premium fashion.

Brand world

NU KAWA positions leather as heritage, tactility, and slow-value craft. The project develops the brand through concept writing, market analysis, client profiling, identity cues, and a mood-driven campaign language.

Positioning

Conscious luxury that feels intimate rather than loud.

The brand story focuses on natural leather, artisanal making, durability, and elegance. Instead of building value through logos, it builds value through material truth and presence.

Client profile

The imagined user is urban, fashion-aware, and drawn to craftsmanship, individuality, sustainability, and premium objects that feel personal rather than disposable.

Brand voice

NU KAWA moves between editorial styling, tactile imagery, and product intimacy. It treats luxury as something lived with, carried, and aged over time.
