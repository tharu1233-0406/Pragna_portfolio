Project 03 / Kidswear

Oceanic Capsule

A gender-neutral kidswear capsule for ages four to eight, shaped by marine life, calm blue tonality, easy movement, and the tactile softness required for children's everyday wear.

Brief

The collection leans toward boys' silhouettes while keeping the language cohesive and playful. The project treats comfort and durability as non-negotiable rather than secondary styling concerns.

Movement

Play comes first, so the garments stay simple, soft, and active.

Hoods, flexible cuffs, raglan sleeves, and washable knits create easy garments that can handle movement, repetition, and the physical pace of childhood without feeling stiff or precious.

Fabric choice

Melange jersey, single jersey, rib knit, terry knit, and full-needle rib were selected for softness, stretch, breathability, absorbency, and shape retention.

Visual mood

The collection draws from ocean calm rather than spectacle, using marine references in a grounded, child-friendly way through shape, print, and quiet color.
