Project 06 / Menswear brand

Alder & Ash

A premium menswear concept rooted in quiet luxury, natural fibers, ethical sourcing, and a wardrobe strategy for young professionals who value substance over display.

Idea

Alder & Ash frames timeless elegance through nature, restraint, and lasting investment value. The collection was developed not only as design, but as a business and brand system.

Brand structure

A menswear label imagined from concept board to commercial strategy.

The project includes identity direction, customer mapping, material planning, pricing logic, SWOT analysis, and digital-first marketing, all tied together through a quiet-luxury aesthetic.

Customer

The target audience is the conscious professional: urban, design-aware, and willing to invest in ethically sourced garments with refined understatement.

Visual language

Earth-driven neutrals, olive tonality, tactile references, and careful tailoring shape a brand that feels calm, assured, and intentionally mature.
