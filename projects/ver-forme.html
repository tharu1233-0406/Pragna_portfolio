Project 02 / Semi-formal

Ver Forme

Ver Forme is a sustainable semi-formal clothing concept for Gen Z. It explores how structured dressing can feel clean, adaptable, and responsible without losing a polished fashion identity.

Audience

The collection is positioned for urban, globally connected young adults who want versatility, transparency, ethical production, and garments that move between settings without looking overdesigned.

Collection thinking

Clean lines with an eco-conscious wardrobe logic.

The project combines export-house precision with modular forms, restrained styling, and a wardrobe structure shaped by sustainability rather than trend excess. It is less about statement dressing and more about smart, repeatable use.

Material approach

Suiting fabrics, organic fibers, recycled inputs, and upcycled materials frame the collection's sustainable direction. The focus stays on drape, structure, density, and longevity.

Retail mindset

Ver Forme was also developed with omnichannel behavior in mind, considering how a collection can serve digital discovery, retail clarity, and adaptable personal styling.
